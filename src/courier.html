Courier Services

Our courier services are designed to ensure efficient transport of agricultural goods from farmers to customers. We offer flexible transport options based on the type, size, and quantity of goods, ensuring safe and timely deliveries.

Courier Service for Farmers

Farmers can use our courier service to deliver their produce to customers. We offer the following transport options based on the size of the shipment:

  • Bike Service - For small goods and quantities.
  • Van Service - For medium-sized shipments.
  • Truck Service - For large goods or bulk produce.

Courier Service for Customers

Customers can opt for delivery of agricultural goods using the following services based on their order size:

  • Bike Delivery - For small-sized orders of fruits, vegetables, or small tools.
  • Van Delivery - For medium-sized orders, including multiple items.
  • Truck Delivery - For large or bulk orders of crops or agricultural tools.
